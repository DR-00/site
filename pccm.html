Cyber Defense

When your digital life is thrown into chaos, when you've been hacked, stalked, locked out of your accounts, or suddenly find your phone and finances compromised, our Personal Cyber Crisis Management service is your lifeline. We act fast to help you regain control, working with you to secure your accounts, restore access to critical services, and stop ongoing intrusions. Our experts guide you step-by-step, from resetting passwords and recovering lost data to identifying and removing malicious software or unauthorized monitoring on your devices.

Beyond immediate recovery, we help you rebuild your digital security and peace of mind.

Includes:

  • Rapid response to account takeovers
  • Investiating hacks, and digital intrusions
  • Guided password resets
  • Secure account recovery
  • Spyware and stalkerware detection and removal
  • Device security checks and privacy hardening
  • Assistance restoring access
  • Forensic investigation of unauthorized access
  • Personalized action plan to stop ongoing threats
  • Support with identity protection
  • Step-by-step guidance to rebuild digital security and peace of mind
  • Education on best practices to prevent future incidents
